STAR WARS

DARTH-VADERS-TIE-FIGHTER

Darth Vader piloted this distinctive experimental TIE fighter above the first Death Star to blast Rebel starfighters into glittering fragments.

FIRST-ORDER_LIGHT-SHUTTLE

Small but hardy, Xi-class shuttles are used by the First Order for tasks ranging from transporting officers between ships to ferrying equipment.

FIRST-ORDER-STAR-DESTROYER

The First Order’s Resurgent-class Star Destroyers boast more efficient turbolasers powered by kyber crystals.

H-TYPE-NUBIAN-YACHT

The H-Type Nubian yacht was a sleek luxury yacht and what it lacked in weapons it made up for in style and a strong deflector shield.

IMPERIAL-INTERDICTOR

A massive and experimental craft, the Imperial Interdictor was equipped with a gravity well. It could pull other ships out of hyperspace, making it a dangerous tool for the Empire.

ZETA CLASS IMPERIAL SHUTTLE

The Zeta-class shuttle is a versatile transport used by the Empire for military and civilian purposes, with a modular cargo pod housed below the holding bay and crew cabin.

NABOO-ROYAL-STARSHIP

The Naboo Royal Starship boasts a strikingly beautiful design that embodies the craftsmanship that prevailed in the peaceful years of the Republic.

REPUBLIC-ATTACK-CRUISER

The Republic attack cruiser was developed and honed for ship-to-ship combat. Its huge armored shape had an aggressive, dagger-like profile, studded with heavy weapons.

THE-RADDUS

Named for a legendary Mon Calamari commander from the early days of the Rebel Alliance, the Raddus served as Leia Organa’s mobile headquarters and flagship of the ragtag Resistance task force fleeing D’Qar.

TIE-FIGHTER

The TIE fighter was the unforgettable symbol of the Imperial fleet. They were single-pilot vehicles designed for fast-paced dogfights with Rebel X-wings and other starfighters.

X-WING-STARFIGHTER

The X-wing is a versatile Rebel Alliance starfighter that balances speed with firepower. Also, it can make long-range jumps with its hyperdrive.

Imperial Star Destroyer

The wedge-shaped Imperial Star Destroyer is a capital ship bristling with weapons emplacements. Its belly hangar bay can launch TIE fighters, boarding craft, land assault units, hyperspace probes, or be used to hold captured craft.
